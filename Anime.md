# Anime

**All Anime** is the parent group that includes every Trash recommended release group, regardless of its format. It is divided into two main subsets:

- **Bluray Tiers**
- **WEB Tiers**

Each subset is further organized into subtiers for more detailed categorization.

## Explanation

- Each term is either wrapped in word boundaries (using `\b`) or in literal character classes (using square brackets) to ensure accurate matching.
- The `|` operator allows matching any one of the listed terms.
- Negative lookahead/lookbehind (e.g., `(?!-raws)`, `(?<!Not)`) are used where necessary to avoid unwanted matches.
